Ensia Library FAQ

How do I use the Catalogue?
To search and reserve books, go to the Catalogue section, enter your search terms (title, author, or keywords), and click on "Reserve" if the book is available.
How does the borrowing process work?
You can borrow books by reserving them online. After confirming your reservation, visit the library to pick them up. Books can be returned in person or via our return drop box.
How do notifications and alerts work?
We send notifications about overdue books and alerts for new arrivals. You can receive these notifications via email or SMS, based on your preferences in your account settings.
How do I manage my account?
To manage your account, log in with your email and password, then access the account settings. You can update your contact information, change your password, or view your borrowing history.
What are the library opening hours?
The library is open from 9 AM to 7 PM, Monday through Friday, and from 10 AM to 5 PM on Saturdays. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.
Can I renew borrowed books online?
Yes, you can renew books online through your account. Log in, go to your borrowed items, and click the "Renew" button for each item you wish to extend.
Are there any late fees for overdue books?
Yes, a small daily fee is charged for overdue items to encourage timely returns. Check the library policy page for the current late fee rate.
Can I suggest a new book for the library to acquire?
Absolutely! You can fill out a "Book Suggestion" form in your account area or contact us directly. We value your input and continually update our collection.
How can I get help with research?
Our librarians are here to help! You can schedule a one-on-one consultation or reach out via the live chat feature during library hours for quick assistance.
